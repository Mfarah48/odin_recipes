Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide pasta sheets with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella,all baked to golden perfection. It's a hearty, flavorful comfort food perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb ground beef or italian sausage
  • medium onion,chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 6 oz can Tomato Paste
  • 1 tsp dried Basil
  • 1 tsp dried Oregano
  • salt and pepper for taste
  • 1-2 tbsp of olive oil

Cheese Mixture

  • 2 cups of Ricotta or Cottage Cheese
  • 1 large Egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tsp dried Parsley

For layering

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles(cooked or oven ready)
  • 3 cups Shredded Cheese

Optional Toppings

  • Fresh Basil
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps Need To Make This

  1. Prepare The Meat Sauce
    • Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Brown the ground beef or sausage.
    • Add onion and garlic; cook untill softened
    • Stir crushed tomatoes, tomato paste,basil,Oregano,salt, and pepper, Simmer for 20-30 minced
  2. Prepare The Cheese Mixture
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta (or cottage cheese), Parmesan, egg, and parsley. Mix until smooth.
  3. Cook The Lasagna Noodles
    • If using regular noodles, cook them in boiling salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Preheat The Oven
